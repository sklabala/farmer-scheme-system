You are a senior software architect, product manager, and full-stack developer.

Build a COMPLETE, real-world, production-ready project based on the following idea.

-----------------------------------
📌 PROJECT TITLE:
Farmer Scheme Information System

-----------------------------------
📌 BACKGROUND:
Farmers lack awareness about many government schemes related to:
- Water irrigation (drip & sprinkler systems)
- Crop seeds purchase
- Rural industries (Grha Udyog)
- Crop damage claims
- Solar panels
- Clean water initiatives
- Drinking water schemes

Even though funds and schemes exist at central and state levels, most farmers only know a few popular ones.

-----------------------------------
📌 SOLUTION:
Develop a unified digital platform where:
- Farmers can register/login
- Browse schemes by category
- Get updates on new schemes
- Receive alerts and notifications

-----------------------------------
📌 OBJECTIVES:
- Provide detailed information on government schemes
- Financial assistance for crops & livestock
- Support vulnerable farmers
- Provide market & commodity insights
- Send disease alerts (plants & animals)
- Promote energy conservation schemes
- Enable Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) awareness
- Improve stakeholder awareness

-----------------------------------
📌 FEATURES REQUIRED:

1. 👨‍🌾 Farmer Module:
   - Registration & login (mobile OTP based)
   - Profile (location, crop type, land size)
   - Dashboard with personalized schemes

2. 📂 Scheme Module:
   - Categories (irrigation, seeds, solar, etc.)
   - Search & filter
   - Scheme details (eligibility, benefits, apply link)

3. 🔔 Notification System:
   - SMS/Push notifications
   - Alerts for new schemes and deadlines

4. 🌐 Multi-language Support:
   - Support regional languages (Hindi, Kannada, etc.)
   - Text-to-Speech functionality

5. 💬 Community Module:
   - Farmer discussion groups
   - Q&A forum

6. 📊 Market Insights:
   - Crop prices
   - Trends and analytics

7. 🐄 Disease Alerts:
   - Plant and animal disease updates

8. 🔌 API Layer:
   - Integration-ready APIs for government systems
